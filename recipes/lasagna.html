Lasagna Recipe

A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

How to make Lasagna

  1. Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl, and set aside.
  2. Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes.
  3. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
