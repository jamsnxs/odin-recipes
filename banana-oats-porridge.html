Banana oats porridge

This creamy sweet banana oat porridge recipe is the best and most delicious served warm with a dash of cinnamon. Mashed banana porridge is easy to make for a quick breakfast rich in protein!
Serve banana bread porridge with nuts, maple syrup, and fresh banana slices.

Ingredients

  • Banana

    • One ripened mashed banana provides natural sweet flavors and creamy textures to this porridge recipe. The banana is rich in potassium and is a great natural energy booster.

  • Oats

    • Old-fashioned oats are simmered on the stovetop and cooked into a thick creamy texture that resembles oatmeal but a little thicker.

  • Milk

    • ou can use any kind of milk for this recipe such as dairy or dairy-free. I used 2% milk and the results were so creamy and amazing!

  • Vanilla Extract

    • Rich vanilla extract provides natural aromatic notes that are stellar and so delicious with the sweet banana.

How to make this recipe

  1. mash the banana with a potato masher until it is smooth and fully mashed.
  2. add the mashed banana, oats, vanilla extract, and milk to a saucepan and bring everything to a rolling boil.
  3. Reduce the heat to simmer and cook the banana oat porridge for three to five minutes until the texture thickens.
  4. Remove the porridge from the stovetop and top with your choice of toppings
