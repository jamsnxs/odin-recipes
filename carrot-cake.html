Carrot Cake

Our top-rated carrot cake recipe is moist, light, fluffy, and topped with a rich cream cheese frosting.
What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • Sugars
  • Oil
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Leavening
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Carrots
  • Pecans
  • Butter and Cream Cheese

How to make Carrot Cake Step-By-Step

  1. Make the batter

    2. Beat the wet ingredients together, then mix in the dry ingredients. Stir in the carrots and fold in the pecans.

  2. Bake the cake

    3. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan or pans. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.

  3. Frost the cake

    4. Beat the frosting ingredients together until smooth and creamy. Stir in the pecans. Frost the (completely cooled) cake.
